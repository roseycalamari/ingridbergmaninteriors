The Person

Founder of Ingrid Bergman Interiors

Ingrid Bergman embarked on her interior design journey in 2013 after spending two decades in the high-end fashion industry. Her time at a renowned interior design firm in Dubai provided her with invaluable experience, allowing her to master the nuances of the trade.

Ingrid's extensive background in retail has proven to be a tremendous asset for her clients. This experience has sharpened her impeccable taste in colors and fabrics and honed her ability to source the most exclusive furniture for her agency.

One of Ingrid's most remarkable qualities is her ability to genuinely listen to her clients, ensuring she delivers the beautiful results they envision. She believes this skill is crucial in building strong client relationships, which lead to inspired, creative design solutions and ultimate client satisfaction.

Ingrid's work is global, with a diverse portfolio of projects. Her home in Portugal, complete with a breathtaking garden, has been transformed into an exclusive showroom where guests can experience the essence of luxury living.

Clients are invited to visit the Villa in the Algarve to explore her collections and discuss their projects in this unique and inspiring setting.

Transforming of a family home

"We recently had the pleasure of transforming a classic traditional American quinta into a refined, modern sanctuary for a young family with three children. This full-scale renovation was more than just a makeover—it was a complete reinvention of the space to align with contemporary living while preserving a warm, welcoming atmosphere.

The home was fully gutted to allow for a bold redesign, featuring an open floorplan that brings in natural light and flow. At the heart of the home stands a stunning, high-end Italian kitchen—both sleek and functional—paired with a custom-designed bathroom that exudes luxury and serenity.

Throughout the house, we laid elegant fishbone oak flooring, adding texture and timeless style. A striking fireplace became a central feature, creating a cozy gathering space for the family. Carefully curated high-end furniture from Eric Kuster, paired with limited edition art and thoughtfully chosen accessories, completed the design with sophistication and personality.

Every detail was selected to reflect comfort, elegance, and a touch of boldness—and the result? A truly elevated family home, perfectly tailored to the lifestyle and vision of our delighted clients."

— Ingrid Bergman

A Bold New Restaurant Concept with Heart

"We had the opportunity to collaborate with an experienced American restaurateur on an exciting new venture: a pub-style restaurant concept with a family-friendly twist. The vision was clear—create a space where you could grab a great steak or burger in a laid-back, stylish setting that welcomes everyone.

From concept to completion, we were involved every step of the way. The standout feature? A stunning 35-meter-long natural stone bar that immediately draws attention and anchors the entire space. Every detail was carefully curated—from material selections and color palettes to the rich flooring textures, statement lighting, and luxurious custom booths designed to comfortably seat groups of up to 8.

This project wasn't just about aesthetics—it was about atmosphere. The result is a warm, inviting, and elevated dining experience that feels both timeless and fresh."

— Ingrid Bergman

Ibiza the magical island

"In Ibiza, we redesigned a beautiful home that captures the island's unique blend of energy and serenity. The interiors feature soft, natural tones that reflect the sunlight and harmonize with the surrounding landscape. We selected sophisticated materials — including organic linens, natural stone, warm wood, and handcrafted details — to bring authenticity and elegance to the space.

The furniture combines relaxed comfort with contemporary design, creating an inviting atmosphere that flows effortlessly between indoor and outdoor living. The result is a stylish, tranquil retreat that celebrates the essence of Ibiza's coastal lifestyle."

— Ingrid Bergman

Apartment upon in the Hills

"In Altea, Spain, we completed a stunning interior transformation of an apartment overlooking the Mediterranean Sea. The design embraces a soft, light color palette that enhances the natural brightness and frames the breathtaking sea views. We chose sophisticated materials — including elegant fabrics, refined wood finishes, and subtle stone details — to create a sense of timeless comfort.

The furniture features clean, contemporary lines with a focus on quality and exceptional comfort, complementing the airy, open feel of the space. The result is a serene, elegant home that blends modern style with the relaxed spirit of coastal living."

— Ingrid Bergman
